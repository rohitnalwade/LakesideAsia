About Lakeside Asia Holdings

Lakeside Asia Holdings is a venture creation platform that builds and scales companies focused on solving critical challenges in human well-being. As a holding company, we provide our ventures with shared expertise, resources, and strategic direction, creating a powerful and efficient platform for them to grow faster and with less risk.

Accelerated Growth

Our platform enables ventures to scale faster by leveraging shared resources and expertise.

Reduced Risk

The holding company structure provides financial stability and strategic oversight.

Synergistic Value

Our ventures create a flywheel of shared expertise, market access, and intellectual property.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

Lakeside Asia Holdings was established with a vision to create a platform for building impactful technology companies.

Initial focus on market research and identifying key opportunities in human well-being sectors.

Launched our first venture, Lakeside Asia Technologies, to serve as the R&D and innovation engine for the group.

2019

First Venture Launch

Established Lakeside Asia Technologies to provide deep-tech consulting services.

2020-2021

Expansion

Launched Lakeside Asia Health and Lakeside Asia Breath Easier to address critical needs in mental health and environmental wellness.

Expanded our portfolio to three distinct but interconnected companies, each targeting multi-billion dollar markets.

Achieved significant milestones including serving 50+ corporate clients and expanding operations across 4 countries.

2022-Present

Growth & Scale

Continued growth and expansion of all portfolio companies with increasing market presence.
