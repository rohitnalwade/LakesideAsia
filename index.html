Lakeside Asia Holdings is a "forever company" structured as a holding entity to incubate, scale, and operate a portfolio of synergistic, high-growth ventures at the intersection of technology, human well-being, and enterprise solutions.

The "Lakeside" philosophy, conceived on the banks of Powai Lake near IIT Bombay, is to build a robust parent organization that provides strategic oversight and operational leverage, allowing its subsidiary ventures to achieve accelerated growth and reduced time-to-market.
